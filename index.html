Swami Vivekananda

Swami Vivekananda was a Hindu monk and one of the most prominent spiritual leaders of India. He was a disciple of Ramakrishna Paramhansa and founded the Ramakrishna Math and the Ramakrishna Mission. Swami Vivekananda is known for his inspiring speeches and teachings on Vedanta and Yoga, and for his contribution to the revival of Hinduism in India.

"Arise, awake, and stop not until the goal is reached."
"In a conflict between the heart and the brain, follow your heart."
"The greatest religion is to be true to your own nature. Have faith in yourselves."
